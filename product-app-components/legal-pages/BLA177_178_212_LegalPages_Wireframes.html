Personal info: name, email, phone, DOB, city, profile photo. Usage data: app activity, check-ins, searches, device info. Location data: GPS coordinates during check-in (BLA-227). Payment data: processed by Razorpay (we never store card details).

To provide and improve the BeLive service. To verify identities via Aadhaar eKYC (BLA-27). To personalize event recommendations. To process payments (BLA-50). To send notifications (BLA-115-119).
